Frequently Asked Questions

What types of accommodations are available on Taniti?

Taniti offers a wide range of accommodations including luxury resorts, family-owned hotels, bed and breakfasts, beach houses, and private condos. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Do I need to book accommodations in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking accommodations at least 3 months in advance, especially during the high season (December-April). For holiday periods and honeymoon packages, 6 months advance booking is advised.

Are there family-friendly accommodations?

Yes, many of our accommodations welcome families with children. Look for the "Family-Friendly" tag in the listing details. These properties often offer amenities like connecting rooms, children's activities, and family meal options.

What payment methods are accepted?

Most accommodations accept major credit cards. Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses also accept euros and yen. We recommend confirming payment options with your chosen accommodation before arrival.
