Yellow Leaf Bay

Yellow Leaf Bay Beach

5.0 (328 reviews)

The most popular beach on Taniti, Yellow Leaf Bay features pristine white sand and crystal-clear turquoise waters. The protected bay offers calm swimming conditions perfect for families and beginner snorkelers.

Beach Details

  • • Type: White sand, calm waters
  • • Family-friendly: Yes
  • • Lifeguards: Yes (9am-5pm)
  • • Best time: Morning (less crowded)

Amenities

  • • Beachside dining options
  • • Water sports equipment rentals

Coral Cove

Coral Cove Beach

4.0 (246 reviews)

A snorkeler's paradise, Coral Cove is famous for its vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful tropical fish. The sheltered cove offers excellent visibility and is perfect for underwater exploration. The beach itself is a mix of sand and small pebbles.

Beach Details

  • • Type: Mixed sand and pebbles
  • • Family-friendly: Yes (older children)
  • • Lifeguards: Seasonal only
  • • Best time: Midday (best visibility)

Amenities

  • • Basic restrooms
  • • Snorkeling gear rentals
  • • Small café
  • • Guided snorkeling tours

Sunset Point

Sunset Point Beach

4.9 (312 reviews)

True to its name, Sunset Point Beach offers spectacular sunset views that attract photographers and romantics alike. This secluded stretch of golden sand is perfect for couples looking for a more private beach experience. The western exposure creates dramatic evening vistas.

Beach Safety Information

Safety Guidelines

  • Always swim in designated areas with lifeguards when possible
  • Check beach flags and warning signs before entering the water
  • Apply reef-safe sunscreen regularly and wear sun protection
  • Stay hydrated and be aware of heat exhaustion symptoms
  • Never swim alone or under the influence of alcohol

Beach Flag System

  • Green Flag: Low hazard, calm conditions, safe to swim
  • Yellow Flag: Medium hazard, moderate surf or currents
  • Red Flag: High hazard, strong currents, high surf
  • Purple Flag: Dangerous marine life (jellyfish, etc.)

In case of emergency, call Taniti Emergency Services at 555-1234 or notify the nearest lifeguard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which beach is best for families with young children?

Yellow Leaf Bay is the most family-friendly beach on Taniti. It features calm, shallow waters, lifeguards on duty, full amenities including restrooms and showers, and easy access to food and refreshments. The soft white sand is also ideal for sandcastle building.

Are there any clothing-optional beaches on Taniti?

No, there are no officially designated clothing-optional beaches on Taniti. Modest beachwear is appreciated, especially when visiting beaches near local villages.

When is the best time to visit Taniti's beaches?

The beaches are beautiful year-round, but the dry season (December-April) offers the most consistent weather for beach activities. For fewer crowds, consider visiting in the shoulder seasons (November or May). Early mornings and late afternoons generally have fewer visitors.

Can I have a beach bonfire or barbecue?

Beach bonfires require a permit from the local authorities and are only allowed in designated areas. Small portable barbecues are permitted on some beaches, but you must clean up completely afterward. Check with your accommodation or the tourist office for current regulations.
