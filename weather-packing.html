Weather & Packing Guide

Taniti's Climate

Taniti enjoys a tropical climate year-round with warm temperatures and high humidity. The island has two main seasons: a dry season and a wet season. Even during the wet season, rain showers are typically brief and followed by sunshine.

Season Months Temperature Rainfall Humidity
Dry Season December - April 75-85°F (24-29°C) Minimal Moderate
Wet Season May - November 70-80°F (21-27°C) Moderate High

Note: During the wet season, brief afternoon showers are common but typically clear quickly. These rarely interfere with vacation plans, and many locals consider this the best time to see the island's lush vegetation.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Taniti depends on your preferences:

  • December - April: Perfect for beach activities and sunbathing
  • May - November: Ideal for seeing lush landscapes and fewer tourists
  • January - February: Peak tourist season with best weather
  • September - November: Lower prices and still plenty of sunshine between showers

What to Pack

Taniti's tropical climate means you'll need to pack accordingly. Here's our recommended packing list to ensure you have everything you need for a comfortable stay:

Clothing

  • ✓ Lightweight, breathable shirts
  • ✓ Shorts or lightweight pants
  • ✓ Sundresses or casual dresses
  • ✓ Swimwear (at least 2)
  • ✓ Light jacket or sweater (for cooler evenings)
  • ✓ Comfortable walking shoes
  • ✓ Sandals or flip-flops
  • ✓ Hat with brim for sun protection

Beach & Outdoor

  • ✓ Sunscreen (reef-safe recommended)
  • ✓ Sunglasses with UV protection
  • ✓ Beach towel or sarong
  • ✓ Insect repellent
  • ✓ Waterproof phone case
  • ✓ Reusable water bottle
  • ✓ Small backpack for day trips
  • ✓ Light rain jacket (wet season)
